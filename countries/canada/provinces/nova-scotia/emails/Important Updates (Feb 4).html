IMPORTANT UPDATES

UPCOMING EVENTS

Since our January 15 meeting & canvassing event went well, we've made it a biweekly occurrence! Our second such event happened on February 1, with somewhat smaller attendance due to biting weather (and aforementioned troubles with email communication) but still an impressive showing.

We are using When2Meet.com to schedule events, to ensure that as many people as possible can attend. Our next upcoming event - where we hope to have a bake sale! - will take place mid-month, sometime between February 14 and February 17. If you're busy at any time, let us know in the poll below, and we'll do our best to accommodate you. Hope you can make it!
VOTE IN OUR MEETING POLL!

EXTINCTION REBELLION HALIFAX!

We've just been made aware that Extinction Rebellion (XR) is holding their first Halifax event on February 8 from 2pm-4pm, in the North Memorial Public Library. Many ES chapters around the world have already allied and partnered up with their respective local XR chapters, and we hope to do so as well.

We plan to get in touch with them very soon (possibly today), and we hope to have as many people from our group as possible in attendance at their event, to represent Earth Strike and begin building this alliance. Hope to see you there on February 8!
CHECK OUT THE EVENT!

OUTREACH UPDATES

As noted in our Supersheet (see below), we have already gotten responses from two prominent local organizations:

  1. A representative from Solidarity Halifax has been in contact with us, and we have an interview with them scheduled for February 6. They are hungry for more information, and we hope our spokesperson (who may or may not be just me) can make a good impression.
  2. The Nova Scotia Public Interest Research Group has also expressed... interest in our cause. We've recently finished filling out their paperwork and are awaiting a formal response (although early signs look good). NSPIRG will be able to help provide us with valuable resources: research opportunities, meeting places, and monetary reimbursements for our ever-growing budget.

Beyond these groups, and others currently on the Outreach page of our Supersheet, we have also recently gotten hold of a comprehensive list of unions in Nova Scotia! This will be polished and updated, and it will serve as valuable reference as we continue to build our support.
